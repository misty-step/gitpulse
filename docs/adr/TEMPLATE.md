## Context

What is the issue? What forces are at play? Why do we need to decide?

Keep this short. If you need more than 3 paragraphs, split the problem.

## Decision

What did we decide? Be specific and actionable.

Use bullet points for multiple related decisions.

## Consequences

What happens because of this decision?

**Good:**
- Benefit 1
- Benefit 2

**Bad:**
- Tradeoff 1
- New constraint introduced

**Neutral:**
- Change that's neither good nor bad

## Alternatives Considered

What else did we think about? Why did we reject it?

Keep brief - one sentence per alternative is often enough.
